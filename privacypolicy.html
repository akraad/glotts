Glotts Privacy Policy

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Glotts mobile application ("the Service") and the choices you have associated with that data.

We use your data to provide and improve the Service. By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information Collection and Use

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service to you.

Data Collected:

  • Email address and User IDs: Collected through Firebase Authentication for creating an account and managing your personalized learning profiles. This is essential for syncing your progress across devices.
  • Purchase history: Collected via the Google Play Billing Library to verify purchases and manage access to premium features.
  • App interactions and User-Generated Content: Includes your learning progress, quiz answers, time spent learning, and any custom word lists (Smart Review Profiles) you create. This data is stored in our Firestore database.
  • Approximate location: Collected via third-party services (AdMob, Firebase Analytics) based on your IP address for analytics and advertising purposes.
  • Device or other IDs: Device IDs (like Advertising ID) are collected by third-party SDKs for analytics and advertising.
  • Crash logs and Diagnostics: Information about application crashes and performance data is collected by Firebase Crashlytics and Analytics to monitor stability.

2. Third-Party Services (Data Sharing)

We use the following third-party services that may collect or share data necessary for the application's functionality, analytics, and advertising:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Analytics, Crashlytics): Used for account creation, cloud database sync of user progress (scores, profiles), and monitoring application performance. Data shared includes User IDs, Email Address, App activity, and Device IDs.
  • Google AdMob (Advertising): Used to display advertisements. Data shared includes Approximate Location, App activity, and Device IDs for ad targeting and reporting.
  • Google Play Billing Library: Used to process and confirm in-app purchases (Purchase history).

3. Security of Data

The security of your data is important to us. We use industry-standard measures (like **HTTPS/TLS encryption in transit** for all Firebase and AdMob communications) to protect your data. However, remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.
