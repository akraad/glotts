Glotts - Account and Data Deletion Policy

This policy explains how users can request the deletion of their accounts and associated personal data from the Glotts service.

1. How to Request Account Deletion

To request the permanent deletion of your Glotts user account and all associated data, please follow the steps below:

  • Option A: Email Request (Recommended): Send an email to our dedicated support email address: raadixdec@gmail.com.
  • The email subject line **must** be: "Account Deletion Request - [Your Registered Email]".
  • In the body of the email, please confirm your wish to delete your account and the email address associated with your Glotts account.

Your request will be handled by our support team, and deletion will be processed manually after identity verification.

2. What Data is Deleted?

Upon successful verification of your request, Glotts will permanently delete the following data associated with your account from our servers (Firebase Firestore) and internal databases:

  • Authentication Data: Your registered email address and user ID (UID) from Firebase Authentication.
  • Learning Progress: All progress data, including your Review Progress (word scores, streak counts, due dates).
  • Learning Profiles: All custom learning profiles you have created.
  • Smart Review Data: Any custom word lists or smart review profiles you have created.
  • Settings and Statistics: All personalized app settings, user skill levels, learning time records, and review statistics (e.g., streaks, daily reviewed counts).

3. Data Retention and Deletion Period

Once your identity is verified, we will initiate the deletion process. This process is typically completed within **30 days**. We will notify you via email once the deletion is complete.
